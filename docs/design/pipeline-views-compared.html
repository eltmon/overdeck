Four ways to look at the same work

Overdeck has four pipeline/board "lenses." They render the same issues, organized differently, for different questions. (Doc-quality reference — also resolves the "which pipeline is which" confusion.)

Two axes that distinguish them: scope (all projects · one project · one issue) and organized by (lifecycle phase · tracker status · per-issue gates). Keep all four — you reach for a different one depending on what you're trying to understand.

Pipeline (1) and Project Overview (2) are the same swimlane idea at different scopes — (2) just adds the hero/stuck/cost layer. Board (3) is the only fundamentally different organizing axis (status, not phase). Review (4) is per-issue. Keep all four — they're complementary lenses, exactly as you said.
