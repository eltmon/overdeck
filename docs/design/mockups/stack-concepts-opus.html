How to choose

  • Quietest / least effort: A (lane drawer) — what we scoped originally.
  • Most live / operator-cockpit feel: B (status bar) — health is always on screen.
  • Cleanest cockpit: C (header pill) — nothing until you ask for it.
  • Most distinctive: D (topology) — but thin payoff with only 2 containers.
  • Hybrid worth considering: C’s header pill as the entry point that opens A’s drawer content. Glanceable + roomy, no permanent lane space.
