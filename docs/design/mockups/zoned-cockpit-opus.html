The idea & the open calls

  • Whole issue at a glance: no tab is ever hidden — status (Now band), the work (center), the records (right), the agents/activity (left) are all on screen at once.
  • 1. The center is permanently Code. That's the thing you watch most. Agree, or should the center be the Now/Overview and Code move to a zone?
  • 2. Right-zone records (Plan · Discussion · Artifacts) are collapsed cards, Beads open. They expand in place. OK — or should any of these be a pane instead (e.g. Discussion can get long)?
  • 3. Timeline → left Activity feed next to the agents. Good placement, or keep a richer Timeline somewhere?
  • 4. Density: this packs a lot onto one screen. On a narrow window the right zone would drop below the center. Acceptable?
